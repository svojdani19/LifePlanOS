# Founder Decisions Required

Only these decisions require founder direction. The engineering prompt below assumes conservative defaults until changed.

## Decision 1 — Standard of Care scope

**Recommended default:** Standard Life Care Plan mode should present “Clinical Guidance and Future-Care Relevance,” not an opinion on whether prior care met the standard. A distinct optional module may perform Standard of Care analysis.

Choose:

- A. Adopt recommended separation
- B. Keep Standard of Care analysis in every report

## Decision 2 — Source approval requirement

**Recommended default:** All sources supporting major surgeries, lifetime care, attendant care, home modifications, or high-dollar recommendations require explicit planner or physician approval before final export.

Choose:

- A. Require approval for major/high-dollar claims only
- B. Require approval for every displayed source
- C. Do not require source-level approval

## Decision 3 — AI-generated recommendations not documented by a treating physician

**Recommended default:** The system may propose them, but they remain excluded from totals until approved by an appropriate reviewing physician.

Choose:

- A. Recommended default
- B. Never allow such recommendations
- C. Allow planner approval without physician approval

## Decision 4 — Full-text source access

**Recommended default:** Store links and permitted excerpts/metadata; do not copy copyrighted full text unless licensed or open access.

Choose:

- A. Recommended default
- B. License a commercial full-text evidence provider later

## Decision 5 — Firm-specific evidence libraries

**Recommended default:** Firms may maintain approved source libraries, but global system evidence should remain separate and centrally validated.

Choose:

- A. Recommended hybrid model
- B. Firm-only libraries
- C. Global-only library
